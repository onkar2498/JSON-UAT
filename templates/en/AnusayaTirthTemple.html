Shri Kshetra Anasuyatheertha

Dabhoi Marg from Pratapnagar (Barode) Western Railway Line to Daha Mailanwar Chandod (Gujarat) is the nawache area. There is a wide vessel of river Narmada at that place. The front east is 'Karnali area'. 'Oar' river flows through Karnali and Narmadeche, there is Orasangam at that place. Since this confluence is sacred, the bones of dead people are immersed there.

On the western side of Chandod area, there are two villages of Mailawar, Tirawar Shinor Nawache of Narmada river. From Shinor to 'Anasuyatirth-Kshetra Dattasthan' five Malavars are on the arrow of Narmada. There is a temple of Mahasati Sadhvi Dattatreya Mata Anasuya at that place. The front of the temple is the temple of Shri Dattatreya. The name of the priest there is Sane

This place is very holy, nature beautiful and delightful. The soil near Anasuya Mandira gets cured by loving it faithfully, there are experiences of many devotees like this. Also, Anasuya Devi Navsas gets such devotional people's devotion! Gangasaptmila Anasuyakshetri big fair bharto. Every Sunday, Tuesday, Friday or day there is a huge crowd of people for darshan.

Here is a famous dispensary for bloodthirsty people. Many patients get cured here. Free medicines are given for them!
