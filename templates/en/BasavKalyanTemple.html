It is told that after the end of Dwaparayuga, when Kaliyuga started, the five Pandavas along with Draupadi left for Badrinath. Abhimanyu's son Parikshit and his son Janmejay means a big yagya banana. Then a child came out of the sacrificial fire. He was very cheerful and very happy. His attitude of being constantly happy started calling him Sadanand. There is a belief that Sri Sadananda or Roopa was born in the beginning of Kaliyuga under Sridatta Prabhun. Or Sadanandani pleased Shri Shankarla by doing intense penance in his childhood. After that Shankarani ordered him to go west. You will meet with Avdhoots. He will be your teacher and guide. Then Sadanand thought, how can I know them? Then he told that you should roar like 'Aho, Shridatta' after taking every thousand steps. Wherever you get a response, you just do penance. There will be darshan of Tula Avdhoot. As per the order of Shankara, Shri Sadanand started moving towards the west. Dattatreya's gazelles would have been done for every thousand Pavalvanars. Basavakalyan came to the city by roaming around like this. Or at some place Shridattatreya's response was received by him. Such a story goes to Sangitali. He started penance there. There he had darshan of Shri Dattatreya and he showered full blessings on Sadanandavar. He established the Ananda Sampradaya at Kalyan. Sri Ramanand, the disciple of Sri Sadanand, has extended this sect further. That tradition is still going on. Or the guru tradition of the sect, Vishnu - Vidhi - Atri - Datta - Sadananda - Ramananda goes on like this.
