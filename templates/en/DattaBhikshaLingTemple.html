Sri Kshetra Dattabhikshalinga Temple

Sri Dutt Bhikshalinga Mandir in Kolhapur City Sunday Pethetil Azad Chowk is Jagrut Dutt Sthan and is considered to be an important place or area since ancient times. In the afternoon Shri Dattaprabhu Etech Jagdambekdun receives alms. Amongst those local Navnaths, one of the Vatsiddh Navnathanni Shri Duttaprabhuna would have been alms-giving bananas. Also Shri Gorakshanathani Dutt Maharaj's alms field would have started from Shridatta Bhikshalinga place at Jholi. Or the story of Vatsiddhanaganathachi about the place has come in Navnathkathasar Adhyay No.36 and 37.

Shridatta Prabhu receives alms in the middle of the day at Kolhapur in South Kashi Kshetra.

Or the local Shri Gorakshanathanni Shri Samarth Ramdas Swamina would have been the head of Kubdi. Today's gentleman gets Gadavar Pahavayas. Shree Mahalakshmi's mother Adeshavarun Shree Narayan Swamin did penance at this place. Shri Dattaprabhu himself gave him this local darshan. His mausoleum is located in the main Dutt Kshetra Shree Nirhasingh Wadi. Or the presence of Shridatta Prabhunche is felt at the time of local Duparchaya Barachya Aartichaya. As in Gangapur, Sadguru Dattatreya's second human incarnation Shri Nrihasingh Saraswati Swami asks for honey during the afternoon. Similarly, Dattaprabhu himself comes here to beg for alms.

Kolhapurla is accessible by rail and bus only. Buses are available from all over Maharashtra to Kolhapurla.
