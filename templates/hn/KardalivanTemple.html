Karanji's 'Shridatta Prabhuncha Aagol'

Throughout the middle of Tretayugat Margashirsha Purnima, the son of Maharishi Atri and Mahapativrata Sadhvi Anasuya assumed the incarnation of Lord Sridattatreyani. Maharishi Bhrigu is the eldest son among the four Manas sons of Brahmadev, the second Maharishi Angiras, the third Maharishi Atri and the fourth Maharishi Vaikhanas! Maharishi Atri and Maharishi Angiras are considered to be the place of Saptarshimadhehi. Kardammuni and Devahuti's Sukanya, the son of Sri Lakshmi's mother, that is Maharishi Atrinchi's wife Mahasadhvi Mata Anasuya! Seeing Atrimuni's knowledge, power of penance and quietness, Kardammuni-Devahutihi was pleased with him and got his Anasuya or Saundaryavati-Susheel daughter married to Atrimuni. 'Anasuya' means 'without Asuya'! Ashya or the great sati Shree Anasuyechan Maher means Shree Datta Prabhuncha Aagol (Maharshi Kardammuni's ashram). Dindori near 'Karanji' or Gavi, a very picturesque complex of Dandakarna, just across the Nashik-Vani road! On the way to Vanikde, Ozharkhed Dam is on the left side and only 3-4 km from the main road on the right side is the holy place. Yalach is called 'Nirjal Math' Asanhi. Parashar, Markandeya, Kanva or ascetic Maharishi would have had ashrams in this area. 'Siddhaana Kapilo MuniH' is described as 'Siddhaanaan Kapilo MuniH', because Kapila Muni did not do local penance.

Shridatta Prabhunchi Padmasan's idol is not found anywhere else and only one idol is found here in India. The actual idol of Sridatta Prabhun of Gangamain has been given as Prasad to Sri Shivdayal Swami who did penance here. In the center of the temple, in the center of the Devgharat, there is a pure white idol of only one height.

Another surprise here is that all the dogs in the area of Pahayala Mitalan gather at 12 noon at Artila Devlachaya's womb and go away after the Aarti.
